The Hoolie cafe @ Rye watersports serves up delicious fresh and tasty homemade food.
All our yummy ingredients are locally sourced; We get sourdough bread from the 'Lighthouse Bakery' in Bodiam, local cheeses from 'Parkers' based in Rye Harbour, and meat from the famous Jamie Wickens @ 'The Ship' in Winchelsea...

Its all about the coffee!

We source our coffee beans from 'Smallbatch' roastery based in Brighton.

You can come and hang out by the lake, where you can drink in the view of the ancient town of Rye overlooking the water. Or you can get active and take a paddle board or windsurfer out to work up an apetite for lunch.
